Mobile

  • A mobile phone, cellular phone, cell phone, cellphone, handphone, or hand phone, sometimes shortened to simply mobile, cell or just phone.


  • A mobile phone is a portable telephone that can make and receive calls over a radio frequency link while the user is moving within a telephone service area.


  • Mobile phones offering only those capabilities are known as feature phones; mobile phones which offer greatly advanced computing capabilities are referred to as smartphones.
